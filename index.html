About Us

Closet Capsule – Where Style Meets Sustainability at Unbeatable Prices.

Closet Capsule is a curated thrift store redefining the way young India shops for fashion. Founded by Mayank Saini, a student with a strong entrepreneurial spirit, the brand was born out of the need to make trendy, branded clothing accessible to everyone — especially those who find retail prices out of reach. Closet Capsule brings together a handpicked selection of pre-loved, high-quality clothing that blends affordability with sustainability. Every piece tells a story and gives you a chance to express your unique style while contributing to a greener planet. What sets Closet Capsule apart is its focus on style, budget-friendliness, and purpose. The store is built for people who want to look great, feel confident, and make conscious fashion choices. By reselling fashion-forward outfits from popular brands at affordable prices, it empowers customers to shop smarter. With ambitious plans to scale, Closet Capsule aims to become a recognized name in the circular fashion space. Through community engagement, online presence, and a strong mission, it is shaping the future of fashion — one thrifted item at a time. Closet Capsule isn’t just a store; it’s a lifestyle for the new-age, eco-conscious shopper.
